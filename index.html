    In my Master's Thesis journey at Mines

    As a Master’s thesis student at the DataDrivenCPS lab , I work under the guidance of Dr. Gabe Fierro , focussing on the accelerating SHACL validation and inference in RDF graphs by treating these processes as dataflow.

    We are approaching this by developing a novel algebra for SHACL validation and inference that facilitates simplification and manipulation of underlying operations as well as opening the door for improved implementation of certain SHACL operations.

    I am keen to engage in meaningful discussions and welcome suggestions or ideas that could contribute to my research. Feel free to contact me.
